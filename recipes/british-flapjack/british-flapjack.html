Simple British Flapjack

This is a simple but tasty flapjack recipe from the UK that is very easy and simple to do for anyone of any skill, even kids.

British Flapjacks are a simple sheet cake with oats, sugar, golden syrup, butter, and raisins.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 tbsp golden syrup
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 5 tbsp raisins
  • 5 oz milk chocolate

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly butter a baking pan.
  2. Step 2: Combine butter, sugar, and golden syrup in a saucepan over low heat. Mix until butter has melted and sugar has dissolved. Remove from heat. Add 2 cups oats and raisins. Mix until oats are well coated. Pour mixture into the prepared pan; flatten down with the back of a spoon.
  3. Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown on top, 10 to 20 minutes. Let flapjack cool.
  4. Step 4: Melt chocolate in a microwave-safe glass or ceramic bowl in 15-second intervals, stirring after each interval, 1 to 3 minutes. Pour over the cooled flapjack. Let cool until chocolate is set.
