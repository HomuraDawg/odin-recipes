Omelette

The True French Omelette

A true French omelette, or omelet as we Americans call it, is just eggs and butter, no filling. The egg is folded for a soft, tender texture.

It's 10% ingredients and 90% technique, so it does take a bit of practice to perfect.

Ingredients

  • 3 fresh eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cold water
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 pinch cayenne or white pepper to taste
